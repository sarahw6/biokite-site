What We Install

Equipment that stays with your facility. Permanently.

⚡

LiFePO4 Battery System

Tested, retired EV batteries. Powers oxygen concentrators, surgical lights, and delivery equipment through outages. Facility-owned.

🌡

Cold Chain Monitor

Continuous temperature and voltage tracking for vaccine storage. Every outage timestamped. Works offline. Audit-ready.

☼

Solar Charging Array

Grid-independent recharging. Battery refills by day, ready when the grid fails at night. No fuel. No generator.

Who We Train

A Certified Energy Officer from your community.

Not an outside contractor. Someone who knows the patients by name. 160-hour certification: battery systems, cold chain, emergency response, and reporting.

Training Includes

  • Battery assembly, installation, and maintenance
  • Cold chain monitoring and compliance
  • Emergency response protocols
  • Weekly scorecard reporting

Certification Outcome

  • Hands-on field exam (pass/fail)
  • Credential recognized by county health authorities
  • Annual recertification requirement
  • Accountable to your facility, not to us
