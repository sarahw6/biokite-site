How It Works

From end-of-life to saving lives.

Your company's retired batteries are tested, refurbished into clinic-grade power systems, and installed in rural health facilities. Every step is tracked. Every outcome is reportable.

1

Collect

Retired batteries collected from your facilities. Tested at WEEE Centre in Nairobi. Cells graded for second-life use.

2

Rebuild

Certified engineers assemble clinic-grade LiFePO4 battery systems. Built locally. Owned by the community.

3

Deploy

Installed in rural clinics. Connected to critical equipment. Solar charged. Oxygen concentrators stay on.

4

Report

Quarterly impact data delivered to your team. Battery diversion volume, CO2 offset, health outcomes. Audit-ready.
