How It Works

From end-of-life batteries to powered clinics.

Retired batteries tested, refurbished into clinic-grade power systems, and installed in rural clinics. Every step tracked. Every outcome reportable.

1

Collect

Batteries collected from your facilities. Tested at WEEE Centre, Nairobi. Graded for second-life use.

2

Rebuild

Certified engineers assemble clinic-grade LiFePO4 systems. Built locally. Community-owned.

3

Deploy

Installed in rural clinics. Connected to critical equipment. Solar charged. Continuous backup power.

4

Report

Quarterly impact data: battery diversion, CO2 offset, health outcomes. Audit-ready.
