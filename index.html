Someone I loved nearly died because the systems meant to keep her alive were fragile. Machines failed. Power failed. Coordination failed. In those hours it became clear — survival depends not just on medicine, but on the invisible infrastructure around it.

That experience changed the course of my life. BioKite Labs exists to solve a problem that is both enormous and strangely ignored.

How it works

Three principles.
One outcome.

01

Reserve

Power is locked to life-saving equipment first and permanently. Oxygen, vaccines, surgical lights never compete with other loads.

02

Own

The clinic owns the system. No contracts, no subscriptions, no dependencies that can be withdrawn. Maintained by a trained energy officer on site.

03

Control

Non-critical loads require deliberate activation. The power reserved for patients is never accidentally consumed.

Where we begin
