Clinic Ready Mode

Three pillars.
One standard.

Opt-out power management: critical equipment is the default. Everything else opts in.

Pillar 1 · Power

LiFePO4 Battery System

A facility-owned power asset. Oxygen concentrators, surgical lights, and delivery equipment stay on through every outage.

  • Facility-owned — the clinic controls it
  • Critical loads always-on by default
  • Non-critical loads opt in deliberately
  • Locally serviceable, no vendor lock-in
Pillar 2 · Monitoring

Cold Chain Monitor

Tracks temperature and voltage through every outage. Vaccines that protect children are never lost to a power failure.

  • Continuous temperature + voltage logging
  • Outage detection with timestamps
  • Works entirely offline
  • Compliance-ready verification
Pillar 3 · People

Certified Energy Officer

Every system is administered by a trained, certified officer on site. Communities control their own health infrastructure.

  • Trained and certified on-site
  • Weekly clinic scorecards
  • Monthly funder reports with KPIs
  • Incident response workflows

Clinical Readiness Protocol

What "clinic ready"
actually means.

A verifiable standard — not a hope. Pass or fail. Every facility, every week.

Required

Always-On Power

Critical loads — oxygen concentrators, surgical lights, delivery room equipment — never lose power during an outage.

Threshold: 0 minutes downtime on critical loads
Required

Cold Chain Integrity

Continuous temperature and voltage logging through every outage. No gaps in the record. No unverified excursions.

Threshold: 100% logging uptime, zero unacknowledged excursions
Required

Certified Officer On Site

A trained, certified energy officer administers the system. Responsible for load management, incident response, and reporting.

Threshold: Active certification, on-site presence
Required

Verified Compliance

Weekly scorecards document uptime, incidents, and response actions. Monthly reports go to funders with KPI dashboards.

Threshold: Weekly scorecard submitted, monthly report delivered
