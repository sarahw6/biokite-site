Why This Matters

Four failures.
One root cause.

01

Unreliable Electricity

~25,000 facilities lack electricity entirely. 70,000+ suffer frequent outages causing equipment failures and spoiled vaccines.

02

Equipment Damage

1 in 3 facilities report equipment damage from voltage fluctuations. ~30% of diagnostic test failures are linked to power problems.

03

Cold Chain Breaks

Vaccine spoilage, reagent degradation, and inconsistent operation of oxygen concentrators and PCR machines compromise patient care.

04

No Documentation

Missing logs, no review cadence, unclear response steps. Stockouts of basic parts drive preventable breakdowns that go unnoticed until too late.

Someone I loved nearly died because the systems meant to keep her alive were fragile. Machines failed. Power failed. Coordination failed. In those hours it became clear — survival depends not just on medicine, but on the invisible infrastructure around it.

That experience changed the course of my life. BioKite Labs exists to solve a problem that is both enormous and strangely ignored.

How It Works

Sense. Detect. Log.
Guide. Report.

1

Sense

Monitors temperature, current draw, and equipment status in real-time.

2

Detect

Identifies outages, undervoltage, and temperature excursions that could harm patients.

3

Log

Creates timestamped proof of all incidents with offline capture via ODK/KoBoCollect.

4

Guide

Provides actionable workflows staff follow to respond to incidents and reduce downtime.

5

Report

Generates weekly clinic scorecards and monthly funder reports with KPI dashboards.
